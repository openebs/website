CONTRIBUTING

We welcome community contributions! You can start with CONTRIBUTING.md or check the CONTRIBUTING.md file in the respective OpenEBS repository for instructions on how to submit changes. If you are planning on making more elaborate changes, please discuss them on the community slack or join our contributor meetings.
